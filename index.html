How to Play
Ancient Totem Duel — Rules
Objective
1
Each player holds a secret objective card listing 3 tikis ranked by point value: 1st = 3pt, 2nd = 2pt, 3rd = 1pt.
2
Maneuver your target tikis to the top 3 slots of the shared totem pole by end of round.
3
First player to reach 21 points on the score track wins the game!
Action Cards — Each player gets these 6 cards per round
⬆️
Up 3
Move any tiki up 3 slots. Tikis above slide down to fill the gap.
⬆️
Up 2
Move any tiki up 2 slots. Tikis above slide down.
⬆️
Up 1 (×2)
Two copies. Move any tiki up 1 slot each.
⬇️
Down 1
Sabotage! Move any tiki down 1 slot to hinder your opponent.
💥
Topple
Send any tiki straight to the very bottom of the pole.
🔥
Toast
Eliminate the bottom tiki permanently. Sent to the Graveyard. Blocked if only 3 tikis remain.
Round Flow
1
Players alternate turns, playing exactly one card per turn.
2
Round ends when all 6 cards each are played OR only 3 tikis remain on the pole.
3
Objectives are revealed. Points are awarded for tikis in the top 3 positions.
4
If both players hit 21 simultaneously, a Bonus Round decides the winner by most points scored in that round.
Scoring Example
If your objective tikis end in positions:
1st
Red Tiki in 1st place+3 pts
2nd
Blue Tiki in 2nd place+2 pts
miss
Green Tiki not in top 3+0 pts
Total this round: 5 pts
